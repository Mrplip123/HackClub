• Tumultuous : Full of chaos, noise, or excitement. Imagine a big, loud crowd at a hockey game or a wild storm!
  • Gracious : Being kind, polite, and respectful to others. For example, saying “thank you” or “good morning” with a smile.
  • Fabulous : Extremely amazing or fantastic! Like when you have the best birthday party ever.
  • Homogeneous : When everything is the same or very similar. For example, a jar full of identical marbles.
  • Ambitious : Having a big goal and working hard to achieve it. Like dreaming of becoming a famous scientist and studying hard to make it happen!
  • Furious : Very, very angry. Like how you might feel if someone accidentally steps on your laptop and breaks it!
  • Enormous : Really, really big. Think of a record-breaking giant pumpkin or a tall skyscraper!
  • Mysterious : Full of unknowns or something that’s hard to explain. It’s like a puzzle or a spooky story that keeps you guessing.
  • Delicious : Tasting super yummy! Like your favorite ice cream or a fresh-out-of-the-oven pizza!
  • Curious : Wanting to know or learn more about something. Like when you keep asking “Why?” or “How does this work?”
