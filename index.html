• Applause : The sound of people clapping to show they enjoyed something, like at the end of a great play or concert.
  • Auction : A sale where people bid money, and the highest bidder gets the item. It’s like a fun competition to see who gets to take something home!
  • Audience : A group of people watching or listening to something, like a play, movie, or concert.
  • Caution : Being careful to avoid danger or mistakes. It’s like looking both ways before crossing the street.
  • Awful : Really bad or unpleasant. For example, stepping in a puddle with your socks on feels ________!
  • Awareness : Knowing or noticing something. For example, wearing a helmet shows ___________ of staying safe while biking. Not peddling into a signpost also shows ___________ of your surroundings.
  • Awakening : Waking up or suddenly realizing something new. It’s like when you discover how much you love a new hobby or subject in school.
  • Aeronautic : Related to flying or building airplanes. Imagine engineers designing cool planes!
  • Audaciousness : Being bold, daring, or not afraid to take risks. For example, trying to skateboard down a big ramp shows _______________!
  • Brawniness : Being strong and muscular. Think of a superhero who can lift really heavy things!
