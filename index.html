• Applause : The sound of people clapping to show they enjoyed something, like at the end of a great play or concert.
  • Auction : A sale where people bid money, and the highest bidder gets the item. It’s like a fun competition to see who gets to take something home!
  • Voucher : A piece of paper or card that can be used like money to buy something or get a discount. It’s like a ticket for free ice cream or a special prize!
  • Foundation : The base or starting point of something. It can mean the strong base of a building or the beginning of a big idea, like the foundation of a new project.
  • Outpouring : A large, sudden flow of something, usually emotions or support. For example, an outpouring of love happens when everyone comes together to help someone in need.
  • Sorrowful : Feeling very sad or full of sadness. For example, you might feel sorrowful if one of your favourite things gets lost.
  • Foreshadowing : A hint or clue about something that will happen later in a story. It’s like when a character says something would never happen, so you know it will happen later on!
  • Scowl : A facial expression that shows you’re annoyed or angry. It’s like giving someone a grumpy or mean look.
  • Dough : A soft, squishy mixture of flour and water (and sometimes other ingredients) used to make bread, cookies, or pizza. It’s super fun to knead!
  • Township : A small area or community, usually in the countryside. It’s like a smaller version of a town.
